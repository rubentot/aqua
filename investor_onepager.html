AquaMonitor

Real-time water quality monitoring for sustainable aquaculture

The Problem

  • Fish farmers rely on manual, infrequent water testing leading to delayed response to quality issues
  • Poor water quality causes fish mortality, disease outbreaks, and reduced growth rates
  • Existing monitoring solutions are expensive and require specialized technical expertise

Our Solution

  • Affordable IoT sensors providing continuous, real-time monitoring of critical water parameters
  • Intelligent alerts and predictive analytics to prevent problems before they occur
  • Simple, intuitive dashboard accessible from any device - no technical expertise required

Market Opportunity

  • Industry increasingly focused on sustainability and technology adoption
  • Regulatory pressure driving need for better monitoring
